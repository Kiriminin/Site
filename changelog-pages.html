Patch Notes Overview:

Patch notes are an essential part of video games and software updates. They provide users with detailed information about changes made to the game or application, helping players stay informed about new features, bug fixes, and balance adjustments. Whether it’s a small update or a major patch, patch notes allow developers to communicate with the community and ensure a better overall experience for players.
